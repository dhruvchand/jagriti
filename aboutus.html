We are students from National Institute of Technology Karnataka, who are looking to help children around the country who are denied of the joys of childhood, by being forced to work, or being subjected to abuse.

This site makes it simple for people to report any case of Child Abuse or Child Rights violations that they have witnessed. The information you provide us will be passed on a suitable organisation, and they will take action based on your complaint.

We work in tandem with multiple NGO's and other Social initiatives to help rid the country of this menace, based on YOUR complaint.
